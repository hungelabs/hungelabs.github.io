If you’re a weird coffee person, you’re probably in the right place. This wiki was created to serve as a hub for all things coffee, from entry-level machine shootouts to custom water.

If you’re new to the whole specialty thing, we have an awesome Getting Started guide that can help you figure out what kind of coffee makes you the most excited.

If you’ve got a great idea (or if you saw a typo!) and you’d like to help this wiki grow, head over to our Contribution page!

This wiki was born out of the James Hoffmann patreon community, but isn’t in any way sponsored or endorsed by Hoffmann. We’re just a bunch of nerds who thought this would be a cool project :) If you want to chat about the wiki but aren’t a Hoffmann patreon, there’s a Hunge Labs Discord you can come hang out in!
